How to make a delicious Peanut Butter and jelly sandwich

This recipe will teach you how to make a quick and easy peanut butter and jelly sandwich

Ingridients

  • sliced bread of your choice
  • peanut butter
  • jelly

Steps

  1. Toast the bread
  2. Spread the peanut butter onto one of the toasted pieces
  3. Spread the Jelly onto the other piece of bread
