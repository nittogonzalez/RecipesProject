How to make a crispy chicken sandwich

How to make a delicious crispy chicken sandwich

Thsi recipe will teach you how to make a delicious crispy chicken sandwich with ingridients you can find in your own kitchen

ingridients

  • Chicken Breast
  • buttermilk
  • paprika
  • garlick powder
  • salt
  • pepper
  • All purpose Flour
  • Eggs
  • Bread Buns
  • Mayo
  • Honey Mustard
  • pickles

Steps

  1. wash the chicken breast and split them in half
  2. prepare the marinate by puring two cups of buttermilk onto a bowl with a tea spoon of salt and a tea spoon of garlick powder
  3. wisk all of those ingridients together
  4. add the chicken breast into the marinate and submerge for at least one hour
  5. prepare the breading by adding 4 cups of all purpose flower onto a bowl, add 2 tea spoons of salt, 1 tea spoon of paprika, and 1 tea spoon of garlick poweder
  6. bring out your breast and coat into the breading we just created
  7. fill up a pan thats big enough to fill up with oil, heat the pan up to 350 degrees farenheit and submerge the chicken
  8. fry for 6 to 10 minutes or until golden brown
  9. toast the bread buns on a separate pan
  10. mix the mayo and the honey mustard
  11. spread some of the mixed mayo onto the bun and add the golden brown piece of chicken as well as 2 to 3 pickles
  12. ENJOY!

this recipe is not supposed to be complicated and should be able to be recreated with ingridients found in any kitchen, please be safe when handling the oil
